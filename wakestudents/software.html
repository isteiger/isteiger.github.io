Some of our Software

Notepad++

An advanced text file editor, easy to use, but with many helpful features. You can zoom in and out, and type in different rows. You can even record a macro, which is a set of actions done by you like typing letters, and have it repeat it!

Snap Camera

A camera editor that lets you put filters over your camera live! If you are familiar with Snapchat, the filters are from them, thus the name. Become a small child, an anime character, or just add a nice background! Useful for Google Meets, just try not to choose one that is too distracting to your classmates!
